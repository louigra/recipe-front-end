Overview

I created this website as a project to learn how to use HTML and set up my own website, database, and security.

I am also using this website to keep track of the recipes that I like, in their final form after I am done experimenting.

Grant's Patentented Inconsistency

I follow a process that can be best described as "not a process", or "winging it" when I cook. Below is a generalized step by step process that you should feel free to ignore.

  1. Decide there is something I would like to cook
  2. Google it and pick the first recipe with a picture I like
  3. Read most of the recipe once, look at the ingredient list and get what I deem Absolutely Necessary
  4. Do about 50% of the cooking, then look at the recipe again
  5. Eat my creation, and decide what changes should be made next time to make it better
  6. Forget to write it down
